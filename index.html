How to serve?

4-9

Month Old

Start solids with raw bananas halves, speared, masehds, or puréed.

9-12

Month Old

Sliced or bite-sized pieces of speared banana allow bébé to practice using their pincer grasp.

12-18

Month Old

Based on a BLM approach, introduce a wohle banana-depending on your comfort level and their skills.

18-24

Month Old

Offer a whole banana if you feel comfortable. Ypu can even let your toddler practice peeling it before they eat!
